Builds

This game mode entices the player to gain these three materials, wood, brick, and metal, usually from removing structures in a player combat environment. The stongest material within builds is metal due to its highest health out of the other materials when building.

Zero Builds

This game mode is more forgiving and less complex for those new to the experience. Instead of gaining materials to build, it provides the player with a 50-over sheild on top of the 100 health and 100 shield.
